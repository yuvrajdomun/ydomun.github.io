I'm Yuvraj Domun

I want to help others turn data into information, and information into insight.

Welcome to my blog!

As a data scientist, I want to build a holistic understanding of data science, and master are all the components required to make data science Work! My personal blog is a space for me to capture and share my data science journey. True to the journey taken by the 'IT' guy in Dan Roberts' book, where the focus is on bringing people, process, technology together to make IT more agile, I want to focus on bringing Data Science (Technology), Data & Operations (People), Data Strategy (Process) together.

Data Science

“We’re at the beginning of a golden age of AI. Recent advancements have already led to invention that previously lived in the realm of science fiction — and we’ve only scratched the surface of what’s possible.” - Jeff Bezos

I am passionate about everything Machine Learning, and Artificial Intelligence (Neural Networks). I have worked extensivly on a wide range of projects both in academia and in the industry to deliver data science solutions. However, the pace at which data science technologies are evolving is unprecedented. In my Data Science journey, I experiment with new techniques and try the cool new toys! My current obsession is the application of Causual Inference on Machine Learning Algorithm.

Data & Operations

“It cannot be changed without changing our thinking.” – Albert Einstein.

DataOps is to Data Science what DevOops is to Software Development. It used to take atleast 6 months to deploy softwares, untill the agile revolution took place. As a Data Scientist, I am very familiar with the long and tedious waterfall process I have to follow to push Data Science Solution to production. But there is room for improvement, I am keen to apply lean princicples to Data Science to improve the velocity at which data science products are productionised. Do we really need to get stuck behind last century's systems and code-first thinking when delivering Data Science?

Data Strategy

“By Failing to prepare, you are preparing to fail.” – Benjamin Franklin.

Everyone wants to to do Data Science. We have heard that hiring an Army of PhDs will not help. In my Journey to understand how to help businesses shape their data Strategy, I focus on understanding the scope of a Data Strategy, what are they key areas organisation needs to focus on when shaping their data strategy, and how they can drive change in a positive direction.
